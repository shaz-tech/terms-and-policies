Account and Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

At Pregneo, we value your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. This page outlines the steps you must take to request the deletion of your account and associated data, and details exactly what happens when you do.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

You can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data through two methods:

Method 1: Directly Inside the Pregneo App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Pregneo app on your device.
  2. Tap on the Settings icon or navigate to your Profile menu.
  3. Select Account Management or Privacy.
  4. Tap the Delete Account button.
  5. Confirm your choice when prompted. Your data will be deleted immediately.

Method 2: Support Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request data deletion by emailing us directly:

  • Email: support@pregneo.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Required Information: Please send the email from the exact Google Account or email address you used to register on the app.

Requests via email are typically processed within 5-7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you initiate an account deletion request, the following data is permanently purged from our active servers:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Pregnancy & Health Data: Current and past pregnancy logs, weight trends, blood sugar logs, hydration records, kick counts, and sleep logs.
  • Media & Journals: All ultrasound photos, bump progress photos, and text journal entries hosted in our cloud storage.
  • Baby Profiles: All registered baby profiles including feeding logs, diaper logs, growth records, and vaccine statuses.
  • In-App Activity: Any personal configurations or settings linked strictly to your user profile.

What Data Might Be Retained?

In accordance with industry standards and legal requirements, we may temporarily retain the following:

  • Financial Records: Anonymous transaction IDs verifying purchases (In-App Subscriptions) may be kept for taxation and auditing purposes as mandated by law.
  • App Analytics & Crash Reporting: Totally anonymized app usage and crash metrics (which are disconnected from your personal identity) are retained simply to ensure app stability.
  • Temporary Backups: Although purged from our active database immediately, your deleted data may remain in encrypted cold-storage backups for up to 30 days before rolling over and being completely overwritten and destroyed.
